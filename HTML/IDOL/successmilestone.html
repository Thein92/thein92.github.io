Award Year Achievement
Myanmar Academy Award for Best Actor 2012 Best Actor award at the Myanmar Academy Awards for his role in the film "Khone Soe" (The Golden Love)
Best Singer Award Multiple times Best Singer awards for his contribution to Myanmar’s pop music scene. These awards have recognized his influence and continued success in the music industry.
Golden Disk Award 2009 The Golden Disk Award is another prestigious award that Sai Sai received, further cementing his status as a leading figure in Myanmar's music industry.
Best Song of the Year Various years Many of his hit songs, including "သူငယ်ချင်းများစွာ " and "ငါ့ရည်းစားက ပိုမိုက်တယ်" have been awarded Best Song of the Year at various local music awards and festivals.
Myanmar Music Award for Best Male Artist 2014 Best Male Artist at the Myanmar Music Awards for his outstanding achievements in the music industry.
Myanmar Entertainment Award 2016 Myanmar Entertainment Award, which is given to individuals who have made a significant contribution to Myanmar's entertainment scene.
Best Social Media Influence 2020 With his huge fan following and presence on social media platforms, Sai Sai was recognized as one of the top celebrities with Best Social Media Influence in Myanmar.
